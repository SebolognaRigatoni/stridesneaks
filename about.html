About Us

Stridesneaks was founded with a passion for redefining urban footwear, blending style, comfort and performance for everyday movement. Our mission is to empower individuals to move confidently through life, one stride at a time. From humble beginnings in small-town Bathurst to a global enterprise of 200 locations, the committed team behind Stridesneaks is proud to deliver the latest trends straight to your doorstep.
